About me

Hello, my name is Xiao Ping (XP). I’m currently at the Singapore-ETH Centre working under a research project on ‘ecosystem services’, which are the benefits that the natural environment can provide to humans. Outside of research, I enjoy playing the drums and going for adventure sports. Feel free to contact me via email or on social media if you’re interested to connect!
