Song, Xiao Ping

Hello, my name is Xiao Ping (XP). I’m currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the National University of Singapore, working on environmental research projects that involve scientists and practitioners. Broadly speaking, my research seeks to incorporate nature and the environment into city planning and design. As an educator, my teaching covers topics ranging from statistical computing and geospatial analysis, to music. Outside of work, I enjoy playing the drums and going for adventure sports. Feel free to contact me via email or on social media if you’re interested to connect.
