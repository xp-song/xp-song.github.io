Song, Xiao Ping

Hello, my name is Xiao Ping (XP). I’m an environmental researcher and educator, with experience in interdisciplinary projects involving scientists, designers and city planners. Broadly speaking, my research seeks to improve the social and ecological performance of urban greenery. As an educator, my teaching covers topics ranging from statistical computing and geospatial analysis, to music. I’m a big fan of reproducible research and workflows, particularly using the R programming language. Outside of work, I enjoy playing the drums and going for adventure sports. Feel free to contact me via email or on social media if you’re interested to connect!

  • Urban ecology
  • Social-ecological systems
  • Data visualisation
