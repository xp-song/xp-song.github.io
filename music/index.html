Hello and welcome!

Music is a big part of my life, and it’s amazing how sound can be used to express different ideas and feelings to the listener. As a drummer, I’m always looking for ways to provide musical texture and to carry the band’s energy in a way that enhances the music we play. As an educator, I’ve also learnt tremendously from my own students. They have taught me to clarify my thoughts, fill in my knowledge gaps, and to communicate clearly.

Some media on my teaching and playing are featured here. I’ve recently been experimenting with audio production… Hopefully I’ll get to share some videos in time to come!
