Academia

Xiao Ping (XP) Song was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the National University of Singapore, jointly appointed at the Department of Architecture and Department of Biological Sciences. His research and teaching looked at sustainability and nature in the context of city planning and design, covering topics ranging from statistical programming to geospatial analysis. He managed projects that operationalized research by developing metrics, machine learning pipelines, and predictive software.
